### What is an Expository Essay?

If you’re unfamiliar with the format, an expository essay is a type of writing that aims to explain, describe, or inform. A good _expository essay_ presents a balanced analysis of a topic based on facts, without the writer's opinion. The core of an expository essay is a **strong thesis statement** , which provides a concise summary of the main point of the essay.
